Thank you for visiting.
I am a doctoral student at the Graduate School of Engineering, Nagoya University. I received my master's and bachelor's degrees from Keio University at Shonan Fujisawa Campus. My current research interests are Affective Computing, Machine Learning, Ubiquitous Computing, and Human-Computer Interaction.
